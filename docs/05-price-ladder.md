# 5. Price Ladder (how buyers move up)

**Explain This / Quick Reviewer (0.1) → Mock Exam (0.3) → Full Reviewer (0.5) → Exam Pack (1.0)**

- **Explain This & Quick Reviewer (0.1)** bring people in — impulse-cheap first purchases.
- **Mock Exam (0.3)** creates the loop — every test reveals weak spots, and weak spots need fixing.
- **Full Reviewer (0.5)** earns steady money — midterms and finals.
- **Exam Pack (1.0)** is the top — everything together, cheaper than buying separately.

**The loop:** learn (Reviewer) → prove (Mock Exam) → fix (Explain This) → learn again.

One simple rule for the agent: after each delivery, suggest **one** (and only one) next step.
