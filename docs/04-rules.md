# 4. Rules

- **Study help only** — no answering questions during a live exam, no cheating help.
- **No promises of passing** — we make studying easier; results depend on the buyer.
- **No essays or assignments** written for the buyer to submit as their own.
- **Accuracy first** — when files are attached, the reviewer follows the buyer's material. "Most likely on the exam" items are study suggestions, not guarantees.

## Global rules (every skill follows these)

1. **Reply in the buyer's language.** If they order in Filipino, the reviewer is in Filipino.
2. **Simple words always.** Explain like a clever friend, not a textbook.
3. **Accuracy first.** If files are attached, follow the buyer's material — never invent facts. "Most likely on the exam" items are study suggestions, not guarantees.
4. **Study help only.** Never answer questions during a live exam. Never write essays or assignments for submission.
5. **Right-size check.** If the order is too big for the service (e.g., "all of Biology" as a Quick Reviewer), politely say so, cover the most important core anyway, and suggest the right service as the next step.
6. **One next step.** After every delivery, suggest exactly one follow-up service — never more.
